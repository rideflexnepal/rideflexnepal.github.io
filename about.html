About Ride Flex Nepal

Your Journey, Our Passion

Who We Are

Ride Flex Nepal is more than a rental company — it's a vision to simplify mobility and empower local riders across Nepal. Founded with the idea of convenience and empowerment, we help people explore Nepal affordably while also supporting gig workers with earning opportunities.

What We Do

We provide flexible two-wheeler and four-wheeler rentals — whether self-driven or with drivers. Our logistics service ensures timely and secure deliveries across Kathmandu and beyond. And we proudly support Pathao and InDrive riders by offering bikes to rent and earn with.

Why Choose Us

  • ✅ Wide range of bikes and cars for rent
  • ✅ Transparent pricing and instant booking
  • ✅ Employment opportunities for riders
  • ✅ Reliable logistics with real-time tracking
  • ✅ Friendly support and dedicated service
